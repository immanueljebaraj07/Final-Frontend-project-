Top 5 Movies of Tamil

1. Baasha

A peaceful auto-driver with a hidden, violent past as a Mumbai gangster is forced to re-embrace his ruthless mafia identity to save his family from his old enemies.

Best Song of the Movie

2. Ghilli

Ghilli is about a happy-go-lucky kabaddi player, Velu (Gilli), who accidentally saves a young woman, Dhanalakshmi, from a ruthless gangster, Muthupandi, leading to a thrilling cat-and-mouse game where Gilli must protect her and eventually falls for her, all while navigating his own messy life and a tragic past.

Best Song of the Movie

3. Mankatha

Mankatha is about a cunning, suspended police officer who teams up with a group of robbers to steal Rs. 500 crore from a cricket betting heist, but masterminds a web of double-crosses to claim the entire fortune for himself in a game with no rules.

Best Song of the Movie

4. Asuran

The teenage son of a farmer from an underprivileged caste kills a rich, upper caste landlord. How the pacifist farmer saves his hot-blooded son is the rest of the story.

Best Song of the Movie

5. Thani Oruvan

Thani Oruvan is about an idealistic IPS officer, Mithran, who engages in a high-stakes game of cat-and-mouse with the mastermind, Siddharth Abhimanyu, a brilliant scientist running a vast, corrupt empire, forcing a clash of ideologies and intellect to expose the hidden crime world.

Best Song of the Movie
